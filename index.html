About Me

I am Kemonaho Christensen Esaie KOH, and I go by Christensen.
My story began in Abidjan, Ivory Coast, where I grew up as the youngest of a family of five children.
I am married and I have a one year old son, James.
My wife and I share a deep passion for education. We both work as English tutors, a role we find incredibly rewarding. There is a special kind of joy in helping others master a new language and watching their confidence grow. It’s a job we truly enjoy, and it keeps us constantly learning alongside our students. These days, I am balancing our teaching work with my studies at BYU-Idaho, where I am majoring in Software Development. My goal is to combine my love for learning with technology to create tools that make a positive impact on people’s lives. In my free time, I enjoy playing some cool games and watching some movies. I also enjoy hiking and camping in the mountains and forest.
